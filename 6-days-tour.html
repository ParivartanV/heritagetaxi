Tour No 1 — 6 Days / 5 Nights — 2 Night Mysore / 1 Night Coorg / 2 Night Wayanad (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Day 1 — Mysore Highlights

Arrive in Mysore and visit Chamundeshwari Temple, Nandi Bull, Mysore Palace, Karanji Lake, Mysore Zoo, and Brindavan Gardens. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Temples & Waterfalls

Visit Somnathpura Temple, Shivanasamudra Falls, and Talakadu temples. Return to Mysore for the night.

Day 3 — Mysore to Coorg

Travel to Coorg via Kushalnagar's Tibetan Golden Temple, Kaveri Nisargadhama, and Dubare Forest. Visit Abbey Falls and Raja's Seat. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 4 — Coorg to Wayanad

Visit Talakaveri, Bhagamandala, and Omkareshwara Temple before heading to Wayanad. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 5 — Wayanad Exploration

Explore Edakkal Caves, Pookot Lake, and Soochipara Falls. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 6 — Departure

Visit Banasura Sagar Dam and Meenmutty Falls before returning to Mysore.

Mysore: Sahara Residency / Krishna Kuteera / Sunflower Hotel. Coorg: Hotel Hill View / Madikeri Heritage / Abbydhama Estate Stay. Wayanad: The Woodlands / Hotel Kallat / Green Gates.
