Two-Day / One-Night Tour Packages

Tour No 1: 2 Days / 1 Night - Mysore

Arrival & Dropping: Mysore

Day 1

Arrive Mysore, Meet assist at the railway station Or Hotel and Later proceed to Chamundeshwari Temple (1000 years old), Nandi Bull Temple, Mysore City View Point, Karanji Lake, Mysore Zoo, Proceed to Brindavan Garden (KRS - 1927 & completed in 1932 ) Night stay in Mysore.

Day 2

Morning breakfast Checkout Hotel proceed to Mysore Palace, Railway Museum, St. Philomina Church ( 5th largest popular Church in India ), Bird Cenctuary ( Ranganathittu ), Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple Srirangapatna, Tippu Gumbaz, Tippu Summer Palace (1000 years old) & go for some shopping before connecting train /Bus for onward journey.

Tour No 2: 2 Days / 1 Night - Coorg (Madikeri)

Arrival & Dropping: Madikeri

Day 1

Arrive Madikeri , Meet assist at the Bus station Or Hotel and Later proceed to Kushalnagar Dalai Lama Golden Temple (Tibetian Golden Temple ), Kaveri Nisargadhama, Dubare Forest (River rafting In boat ) Hysterical Omkareshwara Temple And Madikeri Town Shoping Night stay in Madikeri..

Day 2

Morning breakfast Checkout Hotel proceed Talakaveri (kaveri Rriver Birthplace ) , Bhagamandala ( Triveni Sangam Temple ), Raja Seat ( View Point ), Abbey Falls & go for some shopping before connecting Bus for onward journey..

Tour No 3: 2 Days / 1 Night - Mysore & Shivanasamudra

Arrival & Dropping: Mysore

Day 1

Arrive Mysore , Meet assist at the railway station Or Hotel and Later proceed to Chamundeshwari Temple (1000 years old), Nandi Bull Temple, Mysore City View Point, Karanji Lake, Mysore Zoo, Proceed to Brindavan Garden (KRS - 1927 & completed in 1932 ) Night stay in Mysore.

Tour No 4: 2 Days / 1 Night - Mysore To Coorg (Madikeri)

Arrival & Dropping: Mysore

Day 1

Arrive Mysore , Meet assist at the railway station Or Hotel and Later proceed to Kushalnagar Dalai Lama Golden Temple ( Tibetian Monastery ), Kaveri Nisargadhama, Dubare Forest (River rafting In boat ) Check in your Madikeri Hotel have a break then Proceed to Hysterical Omkareshwara Temple And Madikeri Town Shopping And Night Stay at Madikeri..

Morning breakfast Checkout Hotel proceed to Talakaveri ( Kaveri Rriver Birthplace), Bhagamandala ( Triveni Sangam Temple ) & then Proceed to Raja Seat ( View Sun Set Point), Abbey Falls & go for some shopping before connecting train /Bus for onward journey..

Tour No 5: 2 Days / 1 Night - Bangalore To Mysore

Arrival & Dropping: Bangalore

Day 1

Arrive Bangalore, meet our assistant at the airport, railway station or hotel and proceed to Mysore. En route visit Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple at Srirangapatna, Tippu Gumbaz and Tippu Summer Palace. Check in to your Mysore hotel, refresh and later proceed to Brindavan Garden (KRS Dam, completed in 1932). Night stay in Mysore.

Day 2

Morning breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Chamundeshwari Temple (Chamundi Hill), Nandi Bull Temple, Mysore City View Point, Mysore Zoo, Mysore Palace and St. Philomena Church. Spend time shopping before returning to Bangalore by train/bus/flight as required.

Tour No 6: 2 Days / 1 Night - Mysore To Ooty

Arrival & Dropping: Mysore

Day 1

Upon arrival at the railway station, bus station or hotel in Mysore, meet our assistant and proceed towards Ooty. En route visit Bandipur National Park, Mudumalai National Park, Gudalur Needle Rock Point, 6th Main Shooting Point and Pykara Lake. Check in to your hotel in Ooty and enjoy some local shopping. Night stay in Ooty.

Day 2

Morning breakfast, check out from the hotel and visit the Botanical Garden, Rose Garden, Tea Factory and Wax Museum in Ooty. Do some final shopping before boarding your onward train or bus.

Tour No 7: 2 Days / 1 Night - Mysore To Waynaad

Arrival & Dropping: Mysore

Day 1

Arrive Mysore, meet our assistant at the railway station or hotel and proceed to Wayanad. After checking in and refreshing, visit Edakkal Caves and Soochipara Falls. Night stay in Wayanad.

Day 2

Morning breakfast, check out from the hotel and visit Banasurasagar Dam, Makkiyad (Meenmutty) Water Falls and the backwaters near Kalpetta. Finish with some shopping before connecting to your onward train or bus.
