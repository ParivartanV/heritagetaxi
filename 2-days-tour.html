Curated 2-Day Tour Packages

Pair Mysore's royal heritage with nearby hill stations, waterfalls, and wildlife adventures. Every itinerary is chauffeur-driven and timed for a relaxed two-day escape.

Home / Tour Packages / 2 Days Tour

Plan The Perfect 2-Day Escape

Choose from seven thoughtfully paced itineraries. Whether you want heritage immersion, misty hill towns, or temple trails, these packages include a private cab, experienced chauffeur, and priority sightseeing.

Tour 1

Mysore Heritage Delight (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Mysore

Day 1 Highlights

  • Meet and greet at Mysore railway station or hotel
  • Chamundeshwari Temple (1000 years old)
  • Nandi Bull Temple and panoramic hilltop views
  • Karanji Lake and Mysore Zoo exploration
  • Evening musical fountains at Brindavan Gardens (KRS)
  • Overnight stay in Mysore

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast followed by hotel checkout
  • Mysore Palace and Railway Museum
  • St. Philomena Church (5th largest in India)
  • Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary visit
  • Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple at Srirangapatna
  • Tippu Gumbaz, Tippu Summer Palace, and souvenir shopping
Tour 2

Coorg (Madikeri) Retreat (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Madikeri

Day 1 Highlights

  • Assistance at Madikeri bus station or hotel
  • Kushalnagar Namdroling Golden Temple
  • Kaveri Nisargadhama island and bamboo groves
  • Dubare Forest River Rafting experience
  • Omkareshwara Temple and Madikeri town shopping
  • Night stay in Madikeri

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast and checkout
  • Talakaveri, the birthplace of River Kaveri
  • Bhagamandala Triveni Sangam Temple
  • Raja's Seat sunset viewpoint
  • Abbey Falls nature walk
  • Final shopping before onward travel
Tour 3

Mysore & Shivanasamudra (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Mysore

Day 1 Highlights

  • Meet and greet at Mysore railway station or hotel
  • Chamundeshwari Temple and Nandi Bull Temple
  • City viewpoint, Karanji Lake, and Mysore Zoo
  • Evening stroll at Brindavan Gardens (KRS)
  • Overnight stay in Mysore

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast and hotel checkout
  • Somnathpur Chennakeshava Temple
  • Mudukuthore Mallikarjuna Temple
  • Gaganachukki & Bharachukki waterfalls at Shivanasamudra
  • Talakadu Panchalinga Temples
  • Return transfer with shopping time
Tour 4

Mysore to Coorg Circuit (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Mysore

Day 1 Highlights

  • Pick-up at Mysore railway station or hotel
  • Kushalnagar Namdroling Golden Temple
  • Kaveri Nisargadhama nature park
  • Dubare Forest River Rafting
  • Check-in at Madikeri hotel and refresh
  • Omkareshwara Temple visit and Madikeri evening bazaar

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast and checkout from Madikeri
  • Talakaveri (Kaveri River origin)
  • Bhagamandala Triveni Sangam Temple
  • Raja's Seat sunset viewpoint
  • Abbey Falls photography stop
  • Return transfer with shopping time
Tour 5

Bangalore to Mysore Discovery (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Bangalore

Day 1 Highlights

  • Meet at Bangalore airport, railway station, or hotel
  • Drive to Mysore via Srirangapatna
  • Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple darshan
  • Tippu Gumbaz and Tippu Summer Palace
  • Check-in at Mysore hotel and refresh
  • Evening at Brindavan Gardens (KRS Dam)

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast and checkout
  • Chamundeshwari Temple (Chamundi Hill)
  • Nandi Bull Temple and city viewpoint
  • Mysore Zoo and Mysore Palace
  • St. Philomena Church visit
  • Shopping break before returning to Bangalore
Tour 6

Mysore to Ooty Escape (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Mysore

Day 1 Highlights

  • Welcome at Mysore railway station, bus stand, or hotel
  • Scenic drive through Bandipur National Park
  • Mudumalai National Park wildlife corridor
  • Gudalur Needle Rock viewpoint
  • Sixth Mile Shooting Spot photo halt
  • Pykara Lake boat ride (optional)
  • Check-in and leisurely evening in Ooty

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast and hotel checkout
  • Ooty Botanical Garden and Rose Garden
  • Tea Factory tour and tastings
  • Wax Museum stop
  • Local shopping before onward journey
Tour 7

Mysore to Wayanad Nature Trail (2 Days / 1 Night)

Arrival & Departure: Mysore

Day 1 Highlights

  • Meet at Mysore railway station or hotel
  • Drive through forested ghats into Wayanad
  • Check-in and refresh at Wayanad hotel
  • Explore Edakkal Caves
  • Soochipara (Sentinel Rock) Waterfalls
  • Overnight stay in Wayanad

Day 2 Highlights

  • Breakfast and hotel checkout
  • Banasura Sagar Dam excursion
  • Makkiyad (Meenmutty) Waterfalls trek
  • Backwater viewpoints near Kalpetta
  • Shopping stop before return transfer
