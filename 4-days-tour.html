4-Days Tour Packages

Below are suggested 4-day / 3-night packages with full day-by-day descriptions. Use them as-is or ping us for tweaks.

Tour No 1 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 2 Night Mysore / 1 Night Madikeri (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. Comfortable pace suitable for first-time visitors who want a mix of Mysore city sightseeing and a short Coorg stay.

Day 1 — Mysore city highlights

Arrive Mysore and meet your driver/representative. Start with Chamundeshwari Temple on Chamundi Hill for panoramic views, then visit the historic Nandi Bull. Continue to Mysore Palace to admire its architecture, explore Karanji Lake or the Mysore Zoo if time permits, and in the evening stroll through the illuminated Brindavan Gardens. Overnight stay in Mysore.

Day 2 — Cultural & river-side sights

After breakfast, head to Somnathpur to see the exquisite Chennakeshava Temple, then visit Mudukuthore Mallikarjuna Temple if you prefer offbeat sacred sites. In the afternoon drive to Shivanasamudra to view Gaganachukki and Barachukki waterfalls from the viewpoints. Wrap up with the historic temples around Talakadu and return to Mysore for overnight.

Day 3 — Transfer to Coorg (Madikeri)

Checkout after breakfast and drive towards Coorg. En route visit Kushalnagar's Tibetan Golden Temple, enjoy the peaceful lawns of Kaveri Nisargadhama and take a short boat ride at Dubare (optional river activity). Arrive Madikeri, check in, visit Omkareshwara Temple and explore the local market. Overnight in Madikeri.

Day 4 — Coorg viewpoints & departure

Early breakfast and visit Talakaveri (the Kaveri birthplace) and Bhagamandala (Triveni Sangam), then drive to Raja's Seat for scenic views. Visit Abbey Falls and do last-minute shopping in Madikeri before returning to Mysore or your onward connection.

Hotel Details

Suggested stays — Mysore: Sahara Residency, Krishna Kuteera Serviced Apartment, Sunflower Hotel. Madikeri: Hotel Hill View, Madikeri Heritage, Abbydhama Estate Stay.

Tour No 2 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 1 Night Mysore / 2 Night Madikeri (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. Designed for travellers who want a short Mysore introduction followed by more time in Coorg.

Day 1 — Arrival & evening at the gardens

Arrive and transfer to the hotel; after freshening up visit Chamundi Hill and the Nandi Bull for views, continue to Mysore Palace and spend the evening at the Brindavan Gardens enjoying the fountains and sound-and-light show (seasonal).

Day 2 — Entering Coorg

After breakfast depart for Coorg. Stop at Kushalnagar's Tibetan Golden Temple and Kaveri Nisargadhama. If interested, spend time at Dubare for coracle/boat activities and elephant interactions (optional). Arrive Madikeri for night stay.

Day 3 — Coorg hill stations & temples

Visit Talakaveri early (the river's birthplace) and Bhagamandala (Triveni Sangam), then return to Madikeri for Raja's Seat and local sightseeing. Relax in the evening with local coffee and spice plantation views.

Day 4 — Waterfalls & return

Morning visit to Omkareshwara Temple and Abbey Falls; drive back to Mysore for onward travel. Plenty of time for shopping en route depending on your departure schedule.

Tour No 3 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 3 Night Madikeri (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. Ideal for travellers who want to base themselves in Coorg and explore the region in depth.

Day 1 — Transfer to Coorg

Arrive Mysore and drive straight to Madikeri; on the way stop at Kushalnagar for the Golden Temple and Kaveri Nisargadhama. Reach Madikeri, check-in and relax. Evening at leisure.

Day 2 — Hilltop temples & viewpoints

Visit Talakaveri early morning, then Bhagamandala and Raja's Seat. Enjoy relaxed walks through coffee estates and an optional plantation tour in the afternoon.

Day 3 — Waterfalls & local sights

Visit Abbey Falls, Omkareshwara Temple and Harangi Dam. Spend the evening shopping in Madikeri for spices, coffee and handicrafts.

Day 4 — Wildlife & departure

After breakfast, optional visit to Irupu Falls; if timed right, drive to Nagarhole / Kabini region for an afternoon safari (safari timings vary). Return for onward travel.

Tour No 4 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 1 Night Mysore / 2 Night Madikeri (Arrival & Dropping - Bangalore)

Arrival point: Bangalore. Good for guests arriving into Bangalore who want a combined Mysore + Coorg experience.

Day 1 — Bangalore to Mysore (sightseeing en route)

Pickup from Bangalore airport or station and drive to Mysore. Visit Srirangapatna (Ranganathaswamy Temple, Tipu Sultan monuments) and then proceed to Brindavan Gardens for an evening visit. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Mysore full day

Explore Chamundi Hill, Mysore Palace, St. Philomena's Church, Mysore Zoo and other city highlights. Late afternoon drive towards Coorg/Madikeri and check in for the night.

Day 3 — Coorg excursions

Spend the day at Talakaveri, Bhagamandala and Raja's Seat, followed by visiting Abbey Falls and local viewpoints. Evening is free for shopping and tasting local cuisine.

Day 4 — Coorg to Bangalore (via scenic spots)

Visit Dubare Forest for optional river activities, Kaveri Nisargadhama and the Tibetan Golden Temple before returning to Bangalore for your onward journey.

Tour No 6 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 3 Night Mysore (Arrival & Dropping - Bangalore)

Arrival point: Bangalore. A relaxed itinerary focusing on Mysore city heritage and nearby attractions over several days.

Day 1 — Bangalore to Mysore: city intro

Pickup at Bangalore and drive to Mysore. Visit Lalbagh (if time allows), then continue to Mysore for evening palace visit and local markets. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Temples & waterfalls

After breakfast drive to Somnathpur Chennakeshava Temple and proceed to Shivanasamudra Falls for panoramic views of Gaganachukki and Barachukki. Return to Mysore for the night.

Day 3 — Historical circuit

Visit Srirangapatna (Ranganathaswamy Temple, Tipu's summer palace), Tippu Gumbaz and other historical sites before enjoying Brindavan Garden in the evening.

Day 4 — Final city sights & departure

Finish with Chamundeshwari Temple, Wax Museum, Mysore Zoo and Mysore Palace shopping, then depart to Bangalore or onward destination.

Tour No 5 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 3 Night Madikeri (Arrival & Dropping - Bangalore)

Arrival point: Bangalore. A Coorg-focused package that begins from Bangalore for guests flying into the city.

Day 1 — Bangalore to Coorg

Pickup from Bangalore and drive to Madikeri. En route visit Srirangapatna and Tippu monuments. Arrive Madikeri, check in and relax with an evening stroll.

Day 2 — Coorg classic sights

Visit the Dalai Lama Temple (Kushalnagar), Kaveri Nisargadhama and Dubare. Optional coracle rides and coffee estate visits are available. Overnight in Madikeri.

Day 3 — Hill temples and waterfalls

Early morning visit to Talakaveri, followed by Bhagamandala and Raja's Seat. Afternoon at Abbey Falls and local shopping for spices and souvenirs.

Day 4 — Return journey

Checkout after breakfast and visit Omkareshwara Temple, Harangi Dam and make a final stop at Dubare or local viewpoints before driving back to Bangalore or Mysore for onward travel.

Tour No 8 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 1 Night Mysore / 2 Night Wayanad (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. Great choice to combine Mysore city with two nights in Wayanad's natural settings.

Day 1 — Mysore highlights

Arrive Mysore, visit Chamundi Hill and Mysore Palace in the morning, then relax at Karanji Lake or Lalitha Mahal. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Transfer to Wayanad

After breakfast drive to Wayanad. Stop at Soochipara (Sentinel) Falls and Pookode/Pookot Lake for boating and photo stops. Check in at your Wayanad hotel and enjoy the evening.

Day 3 — Caves, falls & wildlife

Visit Edakkal Caves (early start recommended), Meenmutty Falls and Kalpetta viewpoints. Optionally, book a wildlife safari in Muthanga if available. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 4 — Scenic return

Visit Banasura Sagar Dam and nearby attractions before returning to Mysore for onward travel. Plenty of scenic stops en route for photos and refreshments.

Tour No 9 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 3 Night Wayanad (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. Extended Wayanad itinerary for nature enthusiasts and trekkers.

Day 1 — To Wayanad: Chembra & Edakkal

Arrive Mysore and proceed to Wayanad; visit Chembra Peak viewpoint and Edakkal Caves (timings permitting). Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 2 — Lakes & viewpoints

Visit Pookot Lake, Lakkidi and local tea/coffee estates. Enjoy easy nature walks and local cuisine in the evening.

Day 3 — Trekking & wildlife

Explore Pakshipathalam (birding spot) and Soochipara; consider a safari in the Muthanga wildlife sanctuary if available. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 4 — Water bodies & return

Visit Banasura Sagar Dam and Kuruva Island (boat/permits required) before returning to Mysore or your departure point.

Tour No 7 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 1 Night Mysore / 2 Night Wayanad (Arrival & Dropping - Bangalore)

Arrival point: Bangalore. A route that starts from Bangalore, passes Mysore and ends in Wayanad.

Day 1 — Bangalore to Mysore highlights

Pick up in Bangalore and drive to Mysore visiting Srirangapatna and Tipu Sultan's sites. Visit Mysore Palace and Brindavan Gardens in the evening. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Mysore to Wayanad

After breakfast depart towards Wayanad with stops at Soochipara Falls and Pookot Lake. Check in at Wayanad and relax for the evening.

Day 3 — Wayanad sightseeing

Visit Edakkal Caves, Meenmutty Falls, and local viewpoints like Lakkidi. Option for a short trek depending on fitness and permits.

Day 4 — Return and shopping

Visit Banasura Sagar Dam or Makkiyad before driving back to Bangalore/Mysore for onward travel; stop for local produce shopping if desired.

Tour No 10 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 1 Night Mysore / 2 Night Ooty (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. Great for combining Mysore with a hill-station experience at Ooty.

Day 1 — Mysore: historic start

Arrive and explore Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill and Karanji Lake. Evening drive towards Bandipur/Gudalur region depending on timing, with overnight in a convenient stop or Mysore/Ooty as per plan.

Day 2 — Bandipur / Mudumalai to Ooty

Proceed via Bandipur and Mudumalai, stop at Needle Rock Point and Pykara Lake. Reach Ooty in the evening and explore the local market.

Day 3 — Coonoor day-trip

Take the Nilgiri Mountain Railway (optional) or drive to Coonoor early morning (train timing ~08:45). Visit Sims Park, Lamb's Rock and the tea factory. Return to Ooty for overnight stay.

Day 4 — Ooty sightseeing & departure

Visit the Botanical Garden, Doddabetta Peak, Rose Garden and Ooty Lake. Depart to Mysore or onward destination with scenic views and local shopping stops.

Arrival point: Mysore. An extended stay in Ooty for those who want a leisurely exploration of Nilgiris.

Day 1 — Transfer to Ooty

Drive from Mysore towards Ooty via Bandipur and Mudumalai, stopping for viewpoints and Pykara Lake. Check in at Ooty and relax.

Day 2 — Ooty essentials

Visit Doddabetta Peak, Botanical Garden and Rose Garden. Spend the afternoon at the Tea Factory and exploring local markets.

Day 3 — Coonoor & hill vistas

Day trip to Coonoor via train or car to see Sims Park, Lamb's Rock and Dolphin's Nose; return to Ooty for overnight.

Day 4 — Final Ooty visits & return

Visit Wax Museum, Boat House and Doddabetta (if not done) before departing to Mysore or onward destinations.

Day 1 — Bangalore to Mysore then onward

Tour No 13 — 4 Days / 3 Nights — 1 Night Ooty / 2 Night Kodaikanal (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival point: Mysore. A combined hill-station trip covering both Ooty and Kodaikanal highlights.

Day 1 — Mysore to Ooty via Bandipur

Depart Mysore and drive through Bandipur and Mudumalai, stopping at viewpoints and Pykara Lake. Arrive Ooty, check-in and stroll through the markets or botanical areas.

Day 2 — Coonoor & transfer to Kodaikanal

Take an early drive or train to Coonoor to visit Sims Park, Lamb's Rock and tea estates. Later transfer towards Kodaikanal (longer drive) and check-in on arrival.

Day 3 — Kodaikanal sightseeing

Explore Kodai Lake, Bryant Park, Pillar Rocks, Berijam Lake and Silver Cascade Falls. Enjoy boating, short walks and scenic viewpoints throughout the day.

Day 4 — Final morning & departure

A relaxed morning with optional visits or shopping, then start your return journey to Mysore or your onward destination with scenic stops en route.
