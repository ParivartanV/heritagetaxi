Four-Day Inspirations

Thirteen curated itineraries align with long weekends, festive breaks, and family getaways. Mix-and-match sightseeing, plantation stays, wildlife, and misty hill stations with our seasoned drivers and recommended hotels.

Tour 1

2 Nights Mysore & 1 Night Madikeri

Arrival & Departure: Mysore • Perfect first-time circuit balancing palace grandeur with a Coorg taster.

Day 1 Highlights

  • Meet-and-greet at Mysore and hotel check-in
  • Chamundeshwari Temple and iconic Nandi Bull
  • Mysore Palace interiors and Karanji Lake stroll
  • Evening fountains at Brindavan Gardens

Day 2 Highlights

  • Somnathpur Chennakeshava Temple carvings
  • Mudukuthore Mallikarjuna Temple (optional)
  • Shivanasamudra Gaganachukki & Bharachukki viewpoints
  • Talakadu Panchalinga temple circuit

Day 3 Highlights

  • Transfer to Coorg via Kushalnagar Golden Temple
  • Nature halt at Kaveri Nisargadhama
  • Dubare Forest coracle ride or rafting (seasonal)
  • Madikeri check-in with Omkareshwara Temple visit

Day 4 Highlights

  • Talakaveri (river origin) and Bhagamandala confluence
  • Raja's Seat sunrise or sunset views
  • Abbey Falls photo stop
  • Return transfer to Mysore for onward travel
Stay Ideas: Mysore – Sahara Residency, Krishna Kuteera, Sunflower Hotel • Madikeri – Hotel Hill View, Madikeri Heritage, Abbydhama Estate.

Transparent inclusions and exclusions keep planning simple. Custom tweaks, extra sightseeing, or upgrade requests are just a call away.
