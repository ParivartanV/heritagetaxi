Tour No 1: 3 Days / 2 Nights — 1 Night Mysore / 1 Night Madikeri (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

Arrival & Dropping: Mysore

Day 1

Arrive Mysore, meet assist and visit Chamundeshwari Temple, Nandi Bull Temple, Mysore City View Point, Karanji Lake, Mysore Zoo and Brindavan Garden. Night stay in Mysore.

Day 2

Breakfast, checkout and proceed to Coorg. En route visit Kushalnagar Dalai Lama Golden Temple, Kaveri Nisargadhama and Dubare Forest (boat rafting). Check in Madikeri hotel, visit Omkareshwara Temple and shopping. Night stay in Madikeri.

Day 3

Breakfast, checkout and visit Talakaveri, Bhagamandala, Raja Seat, Abbey Falls before returning to Mysore for onward journey.

Hotel Details

Mysore: Sahara Residency, Krishna Kuteera Serviced Apartment, Sunflower Hotel
Madikeri: Hotel Hill View, Madikeri Heritage, Abbydhama Estate Stay
