Tour No 1 — 7 Days / 6 Nights — Mysore • Coorg • Wayanad • Ooty (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

A varied circuit combining palace heritage, coffee hill-country and western ghats hill stations.

Day 1 — Arrival & Mysore Sightseeing

Arrive Mysore. Visit Chamundeshwari Temple, Mysore Palace, Karanji Lake and Mysore Zoo. Evening at Brindavan Gardens. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Mysore to Coorg

Drive to Coorg via Kushalnagar Tibetan Monastery, Kaveri Nisargadhama and Dubare. Explore Madikeri. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 3 — Coorg Experiences

Visit Talakaveri, Bhagamandala, Abbey Falls, Raja's Seat and local spice plantations. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 4 — Coorg to Wayanad

Drive through scenic routes to Wayanad. Check into resort and relax; evening at leisure. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 5 — Wayanad Highlights

Visit Edakkal Caves, Pookot Lake, Soochipara Falls and tea estates. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 6 — Wayanad to Ooty

Drive to Ooty via scenic Nilgiri passes, visit Needle Rock and Pykara. Overnight in Ooty.

Day 7 — Ooty Sightseeing & Departure

Explore Botanical Garden, Rose Garden and Doddabetta before departing to Mysore or onward destination.
