Seven-Day Signature Circuits

Twelve curated circuits balance palaces, wildlife, coffee country, and Nilgiri hill stations. Pick the ready-made flow that suits your pace or ask us to tweak stays and add activities.

Tour 01

Mysore • Coorg • Wayanad • Ooty

7 Days / 6 Nights · Arrival & Drop: Mysore

Day-by-Day Highlights

  • Day 1: Mysore arrival, Chamundeshwari Temple, Mysore Palace, Karanji Lake, Zoo, and Brindavan Gardens illumination.
  • Day 2: Drive to Coorg via Bylakuppe monastery, Kaveri Nisargadhama, and Dubare elephant interactions.
  • Day 3: Coorg sightseeing - Talakaveri, Bhagamandala, Abbey Falls, Raja's Seat sunset, plantation walk.
  • Day 4: Scenic transfer to Wayanad with relaxed resort evening.
  • Day 5: Wayanad circuit covering Edakkal Caves, Pookode Lake, Soochipara Falls, and tea slopes.
  • Day 6: Misty drive to Ooty via Needle Rock and Pykara cascade stops.
  • Day 7: Ooty botanical garden, Rose Garden, Doddabetta Peak before Mysore drop.
Double check-in confirmations and breakfast buffets included for all three hill stations. Upgrade to premium coffee estate stays on request.

Booking Information

Transparent inclusions, exclusions, and fleet guidance mean you can confirm confidently. Add extra sightseeing, guides, or upgraded rooms anytime.

What's Included

  • Double occupancy rooms with daily breakfast across all destinations.
  • Private AC cab for the entire itinerary, airport / station pick-up and drop.
  • Driver allowance, toll, parking, fuel, and state permit charges.
  • Personalised assistance for scheduling safaris, toy-train seats, or special permits.

Not Included

  • Flight or train tickets, monument entry fees, safari tickets, and camera charges.
  • Lunch, dinner, beverages, and personal expenses such as laundry or tips.
  • Optional adventure add-ons: rafting, off-road jeep rides, zipline tickets, etc.

Fleet & Stay Notes

  • Economy cabs: AC Indica / Etios for 2-3 guests, AC Tavera or similar MUV for 4-6 guests.
  • Standard & deluxe cabs: AC Dzire / Etios for 2-3 guests, AC Innova / Crysta for 4-6 guests.
  • Hotel shortlist shared during enquiry with economy, standard, and deluxe combinations to match your budget.
  • Need early check-in/ late check-out to suit your train or flight? We coordinate with hotels ahead of confirmation.
