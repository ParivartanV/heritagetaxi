Mysore 1 Day Tour Package

Experience the essence of Mysore in a comprehensive single-day tour.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Mysore Palace: Witness the grandeur of the Wadiyar dynasty's official residence.
  • Chamundi Hills: Visit the Chamundeshwari Temple and enjoy panoramic views of Mysore.
  • Mysore Zoo (Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens): Explore one of India's oldest and most well-maintained zoos.
  • Brindavan Gardens: Enjoy the musical fountain show (evening visit recommended).
  • St. Philomena's Church: Admire the neo-Gothic architecture of this historic church.
  • Srirangapatna: Discover the historical island town, including Ranganathaswamy Temple and Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace (optional, if time permits).

What's Included:

  • Private AC car for the entire day.
  • Experienced and courteous driver.
  • Fuel charges.
  • Pickup and drop-off within Mysore city limits.

What's Excluded:

  • Entry fees to monuments and attractions.
  • Food and beverages.
  • Personal expenses.
  • Toll and parking charges (to be paid directly).
