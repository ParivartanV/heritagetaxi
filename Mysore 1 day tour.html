Hour-by-Hour Suggested Flow

Adjust start time, meal breaks, and add-on stops to suit your arrival, group interests, and energy levels.

Morning

Sunrise Pickup & Spiritual Start

Begin with temple blessings and skyline views before the city wakes up.

  • 6:30 AM: Pickup from hotel/railway station in Mysore or Bangalore (additional transfer time applies).
  • Chamundi Hills: Guided darshan at Chamundeshwari Temple, Nandi viewpoint photos.
  • Breakfast: Filter coffee and traditional tiffin at a vetted local eatery.
Driving: 25 km local Ideal for early risers
Midday

Heritage & Living Culture

Dive into Mysore’s royal, colonial, and artisanal quarters with curated guidance.

  • Mysore Palace: Audio guide or chauffeur-led highlights, secret photo angles, silk purchase suggestions.
  • St. Philomena’s: Neo-gothic church interiors and stained glass narratives.
  • Mysore Zoo / Sand Museum: Choose immersive wildlife walk or art stops based on group interest.
Lunch break: 1 hr curated restaurant Entry tickets extra
Evening

Srirangapatna Stories & Garden Lights

Blend history, river islands, handicrafts, and fountain shows before drop.

  • Srirangapatna: Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace, Dariya Daulat Bagh, Gumbaz mausoleum, and river confluence stop.
  • Shopping: Time for sandalwood, incense, and silk bazaars in Devaraja Market.
  • Brindavan Gardens: Musical fountain show seating, night photography, return around 9:30 PM.
Driving: 120 km roundtrip Drop by 10 PM (flexible)

Tour Inclusions & Options

Share pickup location, traveler count, and special requests so we can personalize timings, seating comfort, and add-on experiences.

Inclusions

  • Private AC cab (Sedan/SUV/Tempo Traveller) with experienced chauffeur-guide.
  • Hotel/railway pickup and drop within Mysore city limits.
  • Toll, parking, driver allowance, and interstate permits.
  • Curated sightseeing plan with time suggestions and dining recommendations.

Exclusions

  • Entry tickets, camera fees, and guide charges inside monuments.
  • Meals, snacks, beverages, and personal shopping expenses.
  • Accommodation and Mysore Palace official guide (available on request).
  • Outstation pickup/drop surcharges (Bangalore, Coorg, Ooty).

Popular Add-ons

  • Private palace guide or curated heritage storyteller.
  • Exclusive silk weaving workshop or incense making demo.
  • Evening dinner reservation at rooftop restaurants overlooking Mysore Palace.
  • Extension to Somanathapura or Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary.
