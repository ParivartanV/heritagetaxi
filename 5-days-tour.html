Tour 1

  • Somnathpur's intricately carved Chennakeshava Temple

Day 3 Highlights

  • Checkout for Coorg via Kushalnagar Namdroling Golden Temple
  • Canopy walks through Kaveri Nisargadhama nature island

  • Overnight stay amid coffee estates

Day 4 Highlights

  • Talakaveri origin point and Bhagamandala confluence rituals
  • Scenic ghat drive into Wayanad's evergreen forests
  • Check-in to hillside resort with leisure evening

Day 5 Highlights

  • Edakkal Caves' ancient petroglyphs and viewpoints
  • Meenmutty and Soochipara waterfalls for soft treks
  • Banasura Sagar Dam photo stops and spice shopping
  • Return transfer to Mysore for onward travel

Booking Information

Transparent inclusions, exclusions, and fleet guidance make planning effortless.

Package Includes

  • Accommodation for two adults with complimentary breakfast
  • Arrival, departure, and all sightseeing transfers in private AC cabs
  • Pickup from Mysore airport, railway station, or bus station
  • Driver bata, night charges, tolls, parking, fuel, and permit fees
  • Standard hotel check-in at noon and check-out at noon

Package Excludes

  • Airfare, train fare, monument entry fees, and activity tickets
  • Lunches, dinners, and meals beyond the complimentary breakfast
  • Personal expenses such as tips, laundry, beverages, and phone calls
  • Optional adventures or add-ons mentioned in the itineraries

Fleet Guidance

  • Economy packages: 2–3 guests in AC Indica, 4–6 guests in AC Tavera
  • Standard & Deluxe: 2–3 guests in AC Indigo, 4–6 guests in AC Innova
