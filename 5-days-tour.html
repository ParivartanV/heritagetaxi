Explore our comprehensive 5-day / 4-night packages with day-by-day itineraries across Mysore, Coorg, Wayanad, Ooty and Kodaikanal.

Tour No 1 — 5 Days / 4 Nights — 2 Night Mysore / 1 Night Madikeri / 1 Night Wayanad (Arrival & Dropping - Mysore)

A comprehensive circuit blending Mysore heritage, Coorg coffee country and Wayanad wilderness.

Day 1 — Mysore Royal Trail

Arrive in Mysore and begin with Chamundeshwari Temple atop Chamundi Hill for sweeping vistas. Visit the monolithic Nandi Bull, descend to Mysore Palace to admire its Indo-Saracenic grandeur, stroll Karanji Lake aviary or the famed Mysore Zoo, and close with the musical fountain spectacle at Brindavan Gardens. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 2 — Temples & Waterfalls

Drive to Somnathpur to marvel at the star-shaped Chennakeshava Temple. Stop at Mudukuthore Mallikarjuna Temple, then head to Shivanasamudra for Gaganachukki and Barachukki Falls. Explore the buried temples of Talakadu before returning to Mysore for the night.

Day 3 — Mysore to Coorg

Check out and continue to Coorg via Kushalnagar's Tibetan Golden Temple and the bamboo groves of Kaveri Nisargadhama. Optional rafting or elephant interactions at Dubare Elephant Camp. In Madikeri, visit Omkareshwara Temple and browse spice markets. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 4 — Sacred Coorg to Wayanad

Visit Talakaveri, the revered origin of River Kaveri, then Bhagamandala's Triveni Sangam. Traverse lush forests into Wayanad and settle into your resort amid misty hills. Overnight in Wayanad.

Day 5 — Wayanad Highlights & Departure

Explore ancient petroglyphs at Edakkal Caves, trek to Meenmutty Falls, continue to Soochipara Falls and the scenic Banasura Sagar Dam. Shop for spices and coffee before departing to Mysore.

Hotel Details

Mysore: Sahara Residency / Krishna Kuteera / Sunflower Hotel. Madikeri: Hotel Hill View / Madikeri Heritage / Abbydhama Estate Stay. Wayanad: The Woodlands / Hotel Kallat / Green Gates.
