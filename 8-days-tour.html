Glide between royal Mysore, coffee country, water-carved Wayanad, Nilgiri toy trains, Bandipur safaris, and the cool lakeside charms of Kodaikanal. We arrange relaxed travel days, curated experiences, and local dining suggestions tailored to your pace.

Row across the star-shaped Kodai Lake, hike Coaker's Walk, and taste homemade chocolates before winding back to Mysore or Bangalore.

Day-by-Day Journey Blueprint

We fine-tune timings, experiences, and accommodations once we understand your arrival city, travel companions, and interests.

Day 1

Mysore Welcome & Heritage Trail

Check-in, relax, and ease into Mysore's regal vibe with a curated evening tour.

  • Arrivals: Pickup from Mysore or Bangalore airport/railway station.
  • Highlights: Mysore Palace illumination, Devaraja market spice walk, local dinner recommendations.
  • Stay: Boutique hotel near the palace with 24/7 chauffeur access.
Meal plan: Dinner suggestions provided Driving: 30 km if arriving in Mysore
Day 2

Mysore - Coorg Coffee Country

Drive through sugarcane fields into lush Kodagu and settle into estate life.

  • En route: Stop at Srirangapatna, Tipu Summer Palace, KRS backwaters.
  • Coorg: Abbey Falls, Raja's Seat sunset, optional silk factory stop.
  • Stay: Coffee estate resort with bonfire and plantation views.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 240 km / 5.5 hrs
Day 3

Coorg Plantation Immersion

Relaxed day to soak up estate life, local cuisine, and Kodava culture.

  • Activities: Guided coffee & spice trail, Dubare elephant camp, Cauvery rafting (seasonal).
  • Cuisine: Kodava lunch in a plantation home, honey and wine tasting.
  • Evening: Madikeri fort light show or homestay cooking session.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 80 km local
Day 4

Coorg - Wayanad Waterfalls & Caves

Cross into Kerala's Wayanad for rainforest scenery and tribal heritage.

  • Stops: Irrupu Falls, Tholpetty wildlife range, Kuruvadweep bamboo rafting.
  • Culture: Edakkal caves carvings, local bamboo craft center.
  • Stay: Eco-lodge or treehouse overlooking paddy fields.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 160 km / 4.5 hrs
Day 5

Wayanad - Ooty Nilgiri Escape

Scenic drive up the hairpin bends into the Nilgiris with photo stops.

  • Morning: Soochipara Falls, tea tasting, sunset point at Kalpetta.
  • Nilgiri climb: Hairpin viewpoints, Bandipur roadside safari chances.
  • Evening: Check into colonial-era bungalow or premium resort in Ooty.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 150 km / 4.5 hrs
Day 6

Ooty & Coonoor Toy Train Day

Ride the heritage mountain railway and explore tea estates.

  • Rail experience: Reserved first-class seats on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway (subject to availability).
  • Exploration: Sim's Park, Highfield tea factory, Needle Rock viewpoint.
  • Evening: Boat on Ooty lake or stroll the botanical garden.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 60 km local
Day 7

Ooty - Kodaikanal via Palani Hills

Wind past forests and farm hamlets into the jewel of Tamil Nadu's hill stations.

  • Route: Scenic Ghat roads, 9th Mile, vegetable farms, Palani temple stop if interested.
  • Kodai: Bryant Park blooms, Coaker's Walk viewpoints, evening lake promenade.
  • Stay: View-facing resort or cliffside villa with fireplace.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 255 km / 6 hrs
Day 8

Kodaikanal Highlights & Departure

Slow morning by the lake before descending towards Mysore or Bangalore.

  • Morning: Pillar Rocks, Guna caves photo stop, homemade chocolate tasting.
  • Departure: Drop to Mysore (8 hrs) or Bangalore/Coimbatore airport as per preference.
  • Option: Extend with an extra night in Kodaikanal or Madurai temple tour.
Meal plan: Breakfast Driving: 470 km to Bangalore
