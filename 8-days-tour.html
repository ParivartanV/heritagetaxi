Mysore 8-Days Tour Packages

Home / Tour Packages / 8 Days Tour
Tour 1: 8 Days / 7 Nights (Mysore Start)

Itinerary: 1N Mysore - 2N Coorg - 2N Wayanad - 2N Ooty

  • Day 1: Arrive Mysore, visit Chamundi Temple, Nandi Bull, City View Point, Karanji Lake, Zoo, Maharaja Palace, Brindavan Garden. Night stay in Mysore.
  • Day 2: Proceed to Kushalnagar, visit Golden Temple, Kaveri Nisargadhama, Dubare Forest (Rafting), Omkareshwar Temple. Night stay in Madikeri.
  • Day 3: Visit Talakaveri, Bhagamandala, Raja Seat, Abbey Falls. Night stay in Madikeri.
  • Day 4: Proceed to Wayanad, visit Banasura Sagar Dam, Meenmutty Falls, Lakkidi View Point, Chembra Peak. Night stay in Wayanad.
  • Day 5: Visit Edakkal Caves, Soochippara Waterfall, Pookot Lake, Kuruva Island, Muthanga Wildlife Safari. Night stay in Wayanad.
  • Day 6: Proceed to Ooty, visit Needle Rock View Point, 6th Main Shooting Point, Pykara boat house, Ooty Lake, Rose Garden. Night stay in Ooty.
  • Day 7: Ooty to Coonoor by train. Visit Sims Park, Lamb's Rock, Tea Factory, Dolphin house. Return to Ooty, visit Doddabetta Peak, Wax Museum. Night stay in Ooty.
  • Day 8: Visit Botanical Garden, Mudumalai & Bandipur National Park. Drop at Mysore.
